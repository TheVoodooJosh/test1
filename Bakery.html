About Us

At Kingston Bakes, every treat is made with care, quality ingredients, and a deep love for traditional baking. From crusty artisan loaves to sweet frosted cupcakes, we believe good food brings comfort — and joy — to the everyday.

What began as a weekend hobby quickly turned into a full-time passion. Today, Kingston Bakes is a small-batch bakery focused on handmade breads, pastries, and cakes that taste as good as they look.

Sarah Kingston
